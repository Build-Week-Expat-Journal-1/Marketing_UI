About ExPat Journal

Hello, and welcome to ExPat Journal. We are so happy that you are here! We began this site as a way to chronicle experiences and travels as a digital travel journal. You can save your pictures, thoughts and experiences easily in one convenient place. We simply want to make it easy for you to keep those precious experiences while minimizing your packing as you journey. You can keep it private or you can save it with the world!
